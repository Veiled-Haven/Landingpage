Frequently asked questions

Answers to most common questions.

Why is there an age restriction?

Minecraft is a game that attracts players of all ages, but we believe that by setting an age limit, we can maintain a more mature and responsible community.

What are the means of communication?

We primarily utilize Discord as our main communication platform. It provides a centralized hub for announcements, discussions, and engagement with fellow players.

How is governance of the server handled?

Our server has a dedicated team of staff members who oversee the governance and moderation of the server. They have the authority to take appropriate action against any players who violate the rules, ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

How Vanilla is the server?

We aim to provide a gameplay experience that is as close to vanilla Minecraft as possible. While we may have some additional plugins or features to enhance gameplay, they are carefully selected to complement the core Minecraft experience without detracting from its essence.

How active is the community?

We have a dedicated player base that actively engages in gameplay, discussions, and events. However, it's important to note that we understand that our players have other commitments and responsibilities, which can impact their availability. As a result, activity levels may vary throughout the week or during certain times of the year. Nevertheless, we strive to maintain an engaging and welcoming environment for all players, regardless of their availability.

Is there any Pay to Win crap on this server?

Absolutely not! Our server does not have any Pay to Win elements!

Rules

  1. Follow Discord's TOS
    When on Discord, follow their Terms of Service!: https://discordapp.com/terms & https://discordapp.com/guidelines
  2. Minimum age requirement
    The minimum age requirement for participating on our server is 16 years old. We are committed to maintaining a mature and respectful community, and we believe that a minimum age requirement helps to ensure that our players are able to contribute to this goal.
  3. Respect other players
    Players should treat others with respect and avoid hate speech, bullying, or harassment. This includes any form of discrimination, such as racism, sexism, or homophobia.
  4. No NSFW content
    Sharing of NSFW (Not Safe for Work) content, including but not limited to explicit images, videos, or links, is strictly prohibited on our server. We do not tolerate any form of sexually explicit, violent, or otherwise inappropriate content that could be considered offensive or harmful to others.
  5. No griefing
    Players are not allowed to destroy or modify other players' structures without permission. This includes any form of griefing, such as stealing, breaking blocks, or flooding areas.
  6. Respect Build space
    Don't build too close to other players, always ask before you build near another player. Make sure to leave enough room between your builds to avoid interfering with their work. It is also important to ensure that your own build does not impede on any server infrastructure, such as spawn points, roads, community projects or public areas.
  7. No spamming
    Players should avoid excessive chat messages, commands, Voice chat, or other forms of spamming that disrupt the gameplay experience for others. This includes any form of advertisement or self-promotion.
  8. No advertising
    Players should not advertise other servers or websites within the server. This includes any form of solicitation or promotion of unrelated content.
  9. No cheating
    Players are not allowed to use any hacks, mods, or exploits that give them an unfair advantage over other players. This includes x-ray mods, fly hacks, or any other cheat that allows players to bypass normal gameplay mechanics.
  10. No duping
    The use of any form of block or item duplication glitch/exploit is strictly prohibited! With the exception of duping TNT.
  11. Play fair
    Players should aim to have fun while playing fair and maintaining a friendly, inclusive environment for everyone. This includes avoiding any form of toxic behavior or negative attitude towards other players.
